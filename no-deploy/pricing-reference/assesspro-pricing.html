Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AssessPro licensing.

What exactly counts as a "company" under the Solo licence? +

One company means one assessment target organisation — the company you are contracted to assess. You can conduct unlimited assessments, re-assessments, and follow-ups for that one company. If you need to assess a second company, you would need an Unlimited licence.

Can I upgrade from Solo to Unlimited at any time? +

Yes. You can upgrade at any time and we will pro-rate the difference for the remainder of your billing period. All your existing assessment data, reports, and history are preserved when you upgrade — nothing is lost.

Is there a free trial available? +

Yes — we offer a 14-day free trial for both plans with no credit card required. The trial gives you full access to conduct a complete assessment, generate a report, and experience the platform before committing. Contact us at info@ngstco.com to activate your trial.

Does the Unlimited plan cover all team members in my firm? +

The Unlimited plan is a per-assessor licence. It allows one named assessor to assess unlimited companies. If your team has multiple assessors who each need their own access, each assessor requires their own licence. Volume discounts are available for teams of 3 or more — contact us to discuss.

Which industries does the platform support? +

AssessPro is designed for industrial and manufacturing sectors including oil & gas, petrochemicals, construction, food & beverage, pharmaceuticals, automotive, electronics, and heavy industry. The COSIRI framework applies to all industries; the GMP module is specifically calibrated for manufacturing sectors.

Can I pay annually to save 20%? +

Yes. Annual billing saves 20% compared to monthly billing — that's $480/year saved on the Solo plan and $2,400/year saved on the Unlimited plan. Use the toggle above to see annual pricing. Annual licences are invoiced upfront and are non-refundable after the 14-day trial period.

Do you offer custom enterprise pricing for large organisations? +

Yes. For enterprise deployments, government bodies, large consulting firms with 10+ assessors, or organisations requiring custom integrations, we offer tailored pricing and SLAs. Please contact us at info@ngstco.com with your requirements and we will respond within 24 hours.
